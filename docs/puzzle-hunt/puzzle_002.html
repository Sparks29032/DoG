My favorite sport.

I like to tell my coworkers I’m an athlete since I do quiz bowl. You don’t believe me? Well, the quiz bowl anime is considered to be part of the sports genre, so who are you to disagree?

In fact, I can even say I am a champion in a competitive sport, since I was the top scorer in a tournament. Here is a letter by letter account of how I got to this position in my title competition.

  1. The night before the tournament, I fell asleep at 3 AM to my flashcards.
  2. At the early time of 6 AM, I had to wake up to leave for the tournament.
  3. In our starting round, I, still groggy, accidentally buzzed on the 1st line, but somehow was able to pull the correct answer out of thin air.
  4. Before my next round, I drank 6 shots of espresso to wake myself up.
  5. However, this just caused my hands to jitter, and I accidentally buzzed in early 18 more times.
  6. Luckily, I was able to conjure up the answer line 17 of those times.
  7. This insane run led our team to win our first 5 rounds easily.
  8. At this point, the next best player only had 16 answers within power.
  9. After the lunch break, we played 6 more rounds.
  10. In all 6 of them, I played super aggressive to maintain my lead.
  11. I think I had like 14 powers per round in the afternoon.
  12. The tournament ended at 7 PM, with me claiming my trophy (which was a copy of “The Library of Babel” by Borges) for winning.

I am typing this right after the tournament ended, so I am still a bit woozy. As such, my thoughts may be a bit scrambled.
