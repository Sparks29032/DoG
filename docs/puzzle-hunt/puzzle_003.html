We only have a few bits of information about his program. We can see from our terminal that the random seed of 1908 was used for the program. The terminal also says “[CompLastN1][CompLastN2]”. From our decompiling attempts, we determined that the last output of the program is always the same number, so it is discarded to keep randomness.
